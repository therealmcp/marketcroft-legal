MarketCroft Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

MarketCroft (“MarketCroft,” “we,” “us,” “our”) operates a mobile marketplace application connecting customers with local merchants (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Name and email address
  • Account login credentials and authentication tokens
  • Optional profile information
  • Merchant store and product information
  • Order and fulfillment details

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and language settings
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Usage and interaction data
  • IP address and log data

C. Camera and Media Access

MarketCroft may request access to your device’s camera and photo library to enable features such as:

  • Uploading product images
  • Adding store and profile photos
  • Managing marketplace listings

We only access your camera or photos when you initiate these actions. We do not collect images without your consent.

D. Push Notifications

If enabled, we collect a device notification token to send:

  • Order updates
  • Account alerts
  • Marketplace notifications

E. Payment Information (Stripe)

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. MarketCroft does not store full credit card numbers or sensitive payment data.

F. Location Data (Optional)

If enabled, we may collect location information to support nearby store discovery. You may disable this at any time.

2. Technology Providers

MarketCroft uses the following providers:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and file storage
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Push notification delivery services

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the Service
  • Manage accounts and authentication
  • Enable marketplace features
  • Process payments and orders
  • Send notifications
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share information with:

  • Merchants for order fulfillment
  • Service providers
  • Legal authorities when required
  • Successor entities in business transfers

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with laws, and resolve disputes.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. User Rights and Controls

  • Access and update your account
  • Delete your account
  • Revoke device permissions
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

8. Children’s Privacy

MarketCroft is not intended for children under 13.

9. International Users

Data may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions.

10. Camera Disclosure (Google Play)

Camera access is used solely for capturing and uploading photos for listings and profiles. It is never used in the background.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use indicates acceptance.
